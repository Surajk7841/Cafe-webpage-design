About

Mission Statement: At Food Genda, we are dedicated to serving high-quality coffee, food, and beverages in a welcoming and comfortable environment. We strive to create a community space that fosters connections, creativity, and conversation.

History: Food Genda cafe was founded in 1875 by Gendaswami. Starting as a small coffee cart, the cafe quickly gained a reputation for its delicious coffee and friendly service. Over the years, Food Genda cafe has grown into a thriving community hub, serving a wide range of food and drinks to locals and visitors alike. Today, we remain committed to our founding principles of quality, sustainability, and community engagement.

Values: At Food Genda Cafe, we believe in:

  • Quality: We use only the freshest, highest-quality ingredients in all of our dishes and drinks. Our coffee is sourced from ethical and sustainable farms, and our food is made from scratch in-house.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing, recycling, and energy conservation. We use eco-friendly products whenever possible, and work to reduce waste throughout our operations.
  • Community: We believe that cafes should be more than just places to grab a quick bite or drink. We strive to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere that fosters community connections and promotes creativity and collaboration. We also give back to the local community through partnerships with local organizations and events.

Overall, we are passionate about providing our customers with a memorable experience that they will want to repeat again and again. Thank you for choosing Food Genda Cafe as your go-to spot for coffee, food, and conversation.
